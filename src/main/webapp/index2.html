Hi, I'm Omer

A Software Engineer

I grew up in Ashburn, Virginia. A fun fact about my hometown is that more than half of internet traffic in the US passes through our region. The not so fun fact is that to accomodate this, we have more data centers than there are people.

Software engineering to me is nicely summarized by the phrase "better technology makes more better jobs for horses." It's true that horses did lose their jobs only not to cars, but to other horses driving them, and in some absurd world where horses could drive, I like to think they prefer it and that this is the future I'm working towards with my profession.
