Why Software Engineering?

There are many ways you could go with a computer science degree, but I'm particularly drawn to Software Engineering. The appeal to me is the level of automation-all it takes is for one person to find a solution, and it becomes a click for everyone else.

The Cloud

The cloud made the life of a Software Engineer much easier. Before, deployment looked like finding the infrastructure and hardware to scale your software. Now, that process has been streamlined with many cloud providers only needing you to interact CLI's to provision resources. With this convenience, it seems like the responsibility has fallen closer and closer to the software developer. I wanted to be AWS Cloud Certified to get exposure using real-world tools. I applied what I learned to deploy these projects on the right, please check them out and let me know what you think!
